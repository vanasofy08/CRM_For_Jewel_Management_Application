Project Overview:

Abstract:
    The Jewel Management System is a Salesforce-based cloud CRM application designed to automate jewelry business operations such as managing customers, items, prices, and billing, ensuring data accuracy and process efficiency.
    
Introduction:
    The system replaces manual processes with a digital platform built using Salesforce tools, improving business efficiency, data organization, and customer satisfaction.
    
Objectives:
    To automate jewelry operations, maintain accurate data, improve accessibility, and showcase how Salesforce’s low-code tools can streamline business processes.

System Requirements:
    Software: Salesforce Developer Edition, Chrome/Edge, Trailhead Playground.
    Hardware: Intel i3 or higher, 4GB RAM minimum, 10GB storage, stable internet.

Modules of the System:
    Includes Setup & Configuration, Security & Access Control, Reporting & Analytics, Automation, and User Management modules for structured business handling.

Technologies Used:
    Salesforce Lightning Platform, Flow Builder, App Builder, Schema Builder, and Dashboard Builder for low-code app creation and analytics.

System Design:
    Uses Salesforce’s cloud architecture with objects (Customer, Item, Price, Billing) linked by lookup relationships to ensure seamless data flow and automation.

Workflow Description:
    Covers the complete process—from adding customer and item details to automatic billing generation and real-time updates using Salesforce Flows.

Implementation Process:
    Involves creating Salesforce objects, fields, tabs, profiles, roles, users, dashboards, and flows to bring the system into full operation.

Expected Outcomes:
    Efficient customer and product management, better decisions through analytics, reduced manual work, and improved business communication.

Advantages:
    Centralized data, automation of repetitive tasks, real-time access, easy customization, and enhanced customer experience.

Future Enhancements:
    Mobile app integration, AI-based jewelry suggestions, loyalty programs, chatbot support, and payment gateway integration.

Conclusion:
    The CRM Jewel Management Application provides a scalable, automated, and customer-focused solution that transforms jewelry business operations.
